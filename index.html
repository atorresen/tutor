Hello! My name is AnneMarie.


I have a B.S. in math and an M.A. in teaching math. I’ve been a high school math teacher, a developer of online math education materials, and a grader for the AP Calculus exam.

I am passionate about making math accessible and productively challenging. When I was a classroom teacher, 92% of my students agreed or strongly agreed with “My teacher challenges me to think”… and it paid off! After my first year teaching, my school’s AP Calculus exam pass rate increased by 11%.

I am equally happy working with students who are self-motivated “math people” and students who would rather be doing anything other than math homework. I firmly believe that anyone can learn math and that it can be a joyful and rewarding pursuit.

With experience as an official grader of the AP Calculus exam, I can offer valuable test-taking strategies for high school students hoping to earn college math credit.
